✨ Key Features

🆔
Unique Health ID
Every patient gets a permanent IND-HID identifier under ABDM guidelines.
🔐
Role-Based Access
Patients and doctors have separate secure dashboards with approval workflows.
📋
Structured Records
Diagnosis, Prescriptions, Lab Tests & Procedures in standardized format.
📊
Health Timeline
Lifetime chronological history with color-coded types and smart filters.
⚠️
Abnormal Alerts
Lab values outside normal range are automatically flagged in red or yellow.
📜
Audit Logs
Every record access is logged. Know exactly who viewed your data and when.
📱
Emergency QR
Scannable QR with blood group, allergies and emergency contact info.
🏥
Multi-Hospital
Records from AIIMS Delhi, Fortis Mumbai and more — all unified.
